EtherLocked

PLAY IT

EtherLocked follows the story of a teenage boy who accidentally escapes his body one night. Before sunrise, the player must solve a series of puzzles to guide him back to his body.

Ether Locked is a first-person puzzle game that I developed together with two classmates over the course of a year as a school project. Working in a small team of three gave us the chance to explore every stage of development closely, from concept to final build, while each of us took on multiple roles.

My contributions

  • User Interface design

  • 3D models and textures

  • Outdoor scenery (billboarding)

  • User Experience design

  • Modular environment kit

  • Main menu (UI, UX, programming)
