EtherLocked

Play it!

EtherLocked follows the story of a teenage boy who accidentally escapes his body one night. Before sunrise, the player must solve a series of puzzles to guide him back to his body.

EtherLocked is a first-person puzzle game that I developed together with two classmates over the course of a year as a school project. Working in a small team of three gave us the chance to explore every stage of development closely, from concept to final build, while each of us took on multiple roles.
